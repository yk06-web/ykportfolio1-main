# YK Digitals Branding Guide

This guide contains the core style references required to maintain the YK Digitals brand identity across HTML emails and other external communications.

## Typography (Google Fonts)

Import these fonts using the following URL:
`https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Cormorant+Garamond:wght@400;500;600;700&family=DM+Sans:wght@300;400;500;600;700&display=swap`

*   **Headings (h1 - h6):** `Cormorant Garamond`, serif
    *   **Weight:** 500
    *   **Letter-spacing:** -0.01em
*   **Body Text:** `DM Sans`, sans-serif

## Color Palette

The brand relies on a clean, high-contrast, premium aesthetic featuring crisp white backgrounds, very dark typography, and elegant touches of gold.

*   **Background Color:** `#FFFFFF` (White)
*   **Main Text / Near-Black / Primary:** `#111111`
*   **Gold Accent:** `#C9A87C` (Use for primary buttons, highlights, or links)
*   **Whisper Accent (Lighter Gold/Beige):** `#D9BB9E` (Use for secondary accents or subtle backgrounds)
*   **Dark Section Background:** `#0D0D0D` or `#111111` (Use for dark mode contexts or dark footer areas)

## Additional Styling Details

*   **Border Radius:** Use an `8px` (`0.5rem`) border radius on interactive elements like buttons or cards to maintain consistent shaping.
*   **Link Styling:** Apply the Gold Accent (`#C9A87C`) to inline links to make them stand out elegantly against the white background.
